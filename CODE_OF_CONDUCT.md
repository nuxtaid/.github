# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We expect participation in NuxtAid spaces to be respectful, constructive, and professional.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Be respectful in disagreement.
- Assume good intent, but respond to impact.
- Give actionable, specific feedback.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project or community.
- Be welcoming to people with different backgrounds and experience levels.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory or exclusionary language.
- Repeated bad-faith disruption of discussions or reviews.
- Sharing private or sensitive information without permission.
- Sexualized language or unwanted advances.

## Scope

This applies to repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, and other official NuxtAid communication channels.

## Reporting

To report a concern, contact the organization maintainers through a private channel.

Replace this section with your preferred reporting method, such as a dedicated email address or security contact.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and communications that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent restrictions from participation.
